Jennifer M. Carroll, PhD, LPC

A licensed psychologist with a doctorate in psychology and more than twenty years of clinical experience serving children, adolescents, and adults across Mississippi.

Meet Dr. Carroll

Dr. Jennifer M. Carroll has spent more than two decades in Mississippi mental health, working across private practice, outpatient, inpatient, and forensic settings. A licensed psychologist with a doctorate in psychology, she founded Carroll Professional Services, Inc. in Tupelo and provides therapy, psychological testing and assessment, and court-related evaluations for children, adolescents, and adults.

Her clinical range is broad — from ADHD, autism, and eating disorders to trauma, domestic violence, substance use, and high-conflict divorce and custody matters — and includes forensic work such as competency-to-stand-trial and criminal responsibility evaluations.

Her Approach

Dr. Carroll's approach is individualized and unhurried. She listens carefully, explains clearly, and draws on broad clinical and forensic experience to meet each person's needs — with an emphasis on clarity, professionalism, confidentiality, and respect.
