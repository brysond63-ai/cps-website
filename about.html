About Jennifer Carroll, PhD

Professional, experienced, and compassionate psychological care, offered with clarity, confidentiality, and an individualized approach.

Meet Jennifer Carroll, PhD

Jennifer Carroll, PhD, leads Carroll Professional Services with a deep commitment to thoughtful, professional psychological care. As a PhD-level psychology professional, Dr. Carroll brings experience, careful attention, and a calm, respectful presence to her work with each client.

Dr. Carroll believes that meaningful support begins with truly understanding the person in front of her — their goals, their context, and the questions that bring them in.

Her Approach

Dr. Carroll's approach is individualized and unhurried. She takes the time to listen carefully, to explain things clearly, and to tailor her work to each person's unique needs. Whether providing a psychological evaluation or a professional consultation, she emphasizes clarity, professionalism, and a compassionate, respectful tone.

Professional Values

Dr. Carroll's work is grounded in a clear set of professional values that guide every interaction at Carroll Professional Services:

  • Professionalism
  • Compassion
  • Confidentiality
  • Respect
  • Clarity
  • Individualized care

Commitment to Individualized Care

Above all, Dr. Carroll is committed to care that is tailored to the individual. She understands that no two people are alike, and she works to ensure that each client feels heard, respected, and supported. This individualized, confidential approach is at the heart of Carroll Professional Services.
